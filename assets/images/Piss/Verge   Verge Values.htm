How we do it

  • We are strongly governed by our values. These are what define Verge.

    God Fearing

    We believe that competence to serve comes from the Lord; it comes with the calling of God. God’s call to serve demands dedicated time to prayer and spiritual self-emptying. We dedicate this Business to him through all of these values.

    Continuous Improvement

    We evaluate our internal success by how well we embody these values. They are our identity, they speak on our behalf. Potential and current Employees are evaluated on these values.

    Integrity

    We are fair and honest with each other, our customers, vendors, contractors and the communities in which we work.

    Client Success

    We exceed client expectations through our personalised service, we are flexible and effective at what we do.

    Excellence

    We continually improve our expertise, teamwork, tools and methodologies, we invest in new Technology.

    Accountability

    We make decisions and take actions that contribute to the stability and financial success of our company.

    Collaboration

    We prosper by working as a team, share information with each other in a way that strengthens us and helps us to grow, we freely share important resources, time, knowledge, authority, credit, Reward praise and opportunity and nurture and motivate each other.

    Purpose Driven

    We are a purpose driven company, we believe in having a purpose beyond making profits.

    Trustworthy

    We are Trustworthy. We deliver on our commitments and tell the truth, whichever stage, whichever situation, we admit any mishaps when things go wrong and find immediate solutions, more so, we respect and maintain confidentiality. We are open to clients as to possibilities and impossibilities.
